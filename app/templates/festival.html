Meghalaya – A Land of Festivals and Living Culture

Experience the heart of Meghalaya through its vibrant festivals – the rhythmic Wangala of the Garo Hills, celebrating the harvest with 100 drums; the spirited Behdeinkhlam of the Jaintia Hills, a dance of faith and healing; the sacred Shad Suk Mynsiem of the Khasi Hills, where grace and tradition unite in thanksgiving; and the peaceful rituals of Ri-Bhoi, where nature and community come together in harmony. These festivals are not just celebrations – they are living traditions that echo the soul of the hills, blending ancestral heritage with the joy of the present.
